A New Standard in Long-Life Renewable Infrastructure

Tidal range developments convert the vertical movement of the tide into firm, schedulable electricity. With engineered impoundments, low-head turbines, and reversible generation cycles, lagoon assets can operate well beyond conventional renewable lifetimes.

Reliability

Tidal cycles are astronomically governed – enabling accurate multi-decade output forecasts and complementary dispatch with other renewables.

Longevity

Core civil structures designed for 120–180+ year horizons; modular electromechanical units replaced sequentially for lifecycle optimization.

Scalability

Standardized turbine arrays and adaptive sluice configurations allow replication across suitable macro‑tidal basins.
